Project 2

Over winter break, I took on a solo design sprint to advance my UI/UX skills using Jake Knapp's 5-day methodology. Despite Knapp's recommendation to work with a team, I saw it as a chance to challenge myself—after all, who would want to work during break? Coming from a family of workaholics who struggle with resting, this was a two-in-one opportunity to address my habits while leveraging classroom knowledge in real-world execution.

Long-term Goal

Design a mobile app interface that promotes sustainable and optimal productivity habits

Result

I made a task prioritization prototype based on 3-3-3 productivity system — spend 3 hours on 1 important task, do 3 urgent / shorter tasks, and 3 maintenance tasks.
